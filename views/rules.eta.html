LAUNDRY ROOM

The laundry room has the option of redeeming a key from the manager. A pledge of 50 euros must be paid for the key to the housing company's account:

FI09 2058 1800 0626 43

The message of the payment should be: PESUTUPA, RESIDENT NAME, RESIDENCE

In exchange for a payment receipt, a key can be retrieved from the property manager's office. The deposit is paid back to the resident when the key is returned.

There is no separate charge for the use of the laundry room.

The Laundry room and its equipment may be used between 8.00 and 21.00.

The laundry room is intended for all residents, so it should be left in a neat condition after use.

For more information, contact the property manager's office.



LAUNDRY ROOM INSTRUCTIONS

Make a reservation in the laundry room booking calendar by selecting a date, and entering your own apartment number and start and end time.

Be sure to make a separate reservation for the drying room in the drying room booking calendar.

The dryer is primarily intended for the washing machine operator.

Always clean the dryer and dryer lint filters after use.

Always clean the detergent compartments of the washing machines if they remain messy.

Leave the laundry room clean after use.

If you notice any defects in the laundry room or notice that a machine is out of order, please inform the property manager or maintenance company.



DRYING ROOM INSTRUCTIONS

Please note that there is a separate booking calendar for the laundry room. make a reservation in the drying room booking calendar by selecting a date, and entering your own apartment number and start and end time.

The running time of the drying room is selected by a timer above the drying machine.

During drying, the drying room doors must be closed.

The drying machine can only be used between 8.00 and 22.00

Always clean the fluff filters on the dryer after use.

The dryer in the laundry room is primarily intended for the user of the washing machine (Check the laundry room booking calendar)

Leave the drying room clean after use.

The trash can is located on the side of the laundry room.

If you notice any defects in the laundry room or notice that a machine is out of order, please inform the property manager or maintenance company.
